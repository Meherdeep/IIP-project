What do we want to do for them?

In India local and small scale industries receive little to no support from the government or any other startup entity and hence we see small scale and handicraft industries die out, between competing with foreign good and investing in their business to upgrade their equipment they fall and are unable to survive the market and hence ultimately end up in closure.

Our Technical Aspect

We are working on making a handheld measuring device that functions with the help of an arduino. This once completed will be given to the industries as our bit to help them modernize the techniques. This device is capable of measuring the length of any article just by drawing over it. This will give the workman an exact length and based on this he can proceed forward, such a device will ensure a quality consistency and a less material wastage
